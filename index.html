What is Social Listening?

Social Listening is the practice of monitoring digital conversations across social media, forums, search engines, and review platforms to understand what consumers are saying about brands, categories, and health conditions. It transforms unstructured online chatter into actionable consumer insights.

By analyzing mentions, sentiment, search trends, and e-commerce reviews, we uncover purchase drivers, pain points, unmet needs, and emerging trends that inform brand strategy.

Iterative Experiment Platform
Testing real consumer behavior through Meta landing pages

What is IEP?

The Iterative Experiment Platform is a dynamic testing platform that simulates a product launch by transforming concepts into Meta (Facebook & Instagram) landing pages. It evaluates a product’s ability to grab attention, engage consumers, and drive action through real-world digital behavior.

Each experiment goes through multiple rounds of testing, progressively refining claims, visuals, and targeting to find the optimal messaging strategy.

Stopping Power
How effectively a creative catches consumer attention — measured by Banner Ad Click-Through Rate.
Closing Power
The stage where the shopper places the product in cart — measured by Add to Cart CTR.
